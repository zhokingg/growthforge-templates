Your Journey

From viewing
to move-in day

A clear, transparent process. No surprises, no jargon — just a thoughtful path to your new home.

Week 1
Private viewing
Schedule an in-person tour or virtual walkthrough with one of our residence advisors. Take your time.
Week 2
Application & review
Submit your application online. Our team reviews within 48 hours — typically much faster.
Week 3
Lease signing
Review and sign your lease digitally. We walk you through every clause to ensure complete clarity.
Week 4
Personalisation
Choose from our curated selection of paint colours, fixtures, and optional upgrades before move-in.
Week 5
Move-in day
Your keys, a welcome gift from a neighbourhood artisan, and a dedicated move-in concierge to help settle you in.
