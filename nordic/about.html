Our Story

Born of two cities

Nordic was founded in 2018 by a Copenhagen architect and a New York developer who met on a summer residency in Jutland. They shared a conviction that Manhattan deserved homes made with the same quiet care as the farmhouses of the Danish coast.

Eight years later, we remain a small studio — just twelve people — designing and building a single collection of twenty-four residences in Tribeca. Every decision passes through the same hands. Every detail is chosen twice.

Principles

What we believe

Six ideas that shape every floor plan, every fixture, every finish we choose. They are not rules so much as habits — the quiet disciplines of a studio that prefers to do less, better.

Honest Materials

White oak, linen, wool, stone, unlacquered brass. Materials that age with dignity and reveal their true nature over years of use.

Natural Light

Every floor plan begins with the sun. We study its arc through each window and design rooms around the hours they feel best.

Quiet Details

Hardware you barely notice. Edges softened by hand. Joinery that takes an extra day but lasts a lifetime. Details made to be felt, not seen.

Slow Craft

We work with artisans, not factories. Our cabinetry, tile, and stone come from workshops where one person finishes what they began.

Durable Beauty

Trends age badly. We build for thirty years, not thirty months — rooms that will still feel right when the furniture inside them has changed many times.

Room to Breathe

Generous ceilings, wide thresholds, empty walls. Space is the most valuable thing a home can offer, and we give it away freely.
