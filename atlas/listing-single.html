Modern Colonial on Maple Drive

📍 142 Maple Dr, Westfield, NJ 07090
Single Family 4 Beds 3 Baths 2,850 sqft 0.38 acres Built 2019

Description

Meticulously maintained 4-bedroom colonial in one of Westfield's most sought-after neighbourhoods. This 2019 build features an open floor plan with 10-foot ceilings, custom millwork, and a chef's kitchen with Sub-Zero and Wolf appliances. The primary suite includes a spa-style bath with radiant floor heating and walk-in closet with built-in organizers.

The fully finished basement adds 1,200 sqft of flexible living space with a wet bar, media room, and full bath. Outside, a professionally landscaped yard with bluestone patio, built-in grill, and mature plantings creates an ideal setting for entertaining. Walking distance to downtown shops, restaurants, and the Westfield train station (35 min to Penn Station).
