About Atlas

A modern brokerage built on data, transparency, and deep local expertise. Serving buyers and sellers since 2014.

Our Story

Real estate, reimagined.

Atlas was founded on a simple observation: buying or selling a home shouldn't require guesswork. We built a platform that combines the warmth of a local brokerage with the analytical power of a technology company.

Today, 85+ agents across four states use Atlas to deliver market intelligence, transparent pricing, and concierge-level service to every client.
