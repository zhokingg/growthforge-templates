A six-bedroom cliffside retreat above the Tyrrhenian Sea — Casa del Sol is one of the Amalfi Coast's most quietly storied homes. Tiered terraces, an infinity pool that meets the horizon, and a private path down to a hidden swim cove.

About this villa

Carved into the cliffs above Praiano, Casa del Sol has belonged to the same Neapolitan family for four generations. A two-year restoration completed in 2023 honoured the original lemon-grove terraces, vaulted majolica ceilings and hand-painted Vietri tiles, while adding the comforts a modern luxury escape demands — climate control, a gym, a wine cellar and a screening room tucked beneath the main pavilion.


The setting

The villa unfolds across three garden terraces. The uppermost holds the infinity pool and bar pavilion, with views that stretch from Capri to the Faraglioni. The middle terrace is where most days end — a long olive-wood dining table beneath a pergola of climbing jasmine. From the lowest garden, a private staircase descends 88 steps to a small swim cove with a wooden sun deck and storage for paddleboards.


Inside

Six en-suite bedrooms are spread across two pavilions for privacy. The principal suite occupies its own wing with a marble bathroom, a fireplace and a private terrace. A professional chef's kitchen, a formal dining room for fourteen, and a sun-flooded living room with sea-facing French doors complete the main house.
