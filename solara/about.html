Our Journey

Curating the Mediterranean dream

Solara was born in 2006 when our founders, Elena and Marco Rossi, restored a forgotten lemon grove villa above the Bay of Naples. Friends began asking to stay. Then friends of friends. Within two years, a single property had quietly become a portfolio of seven, each chosen for its soul as much as its sea view.


Today we represent eighty-four homes across twelve coastal regions — from the cliffside terraces of the Amalfi Coast to the whitewashed serenity of Santorini and the pine-scented coves of Mallorca. Every villa is visited, photographed, and approved in person by our team. Nothing is listed sight unseen.


What hasn't changed is the original promise: a home that feels like a secret, served by people who treat your holiday as their own.

Our Values

What makes Solara unique

Four principles guide every villa we accept and every guest we welcome.

Handpicked Properties

Every villa is personally inspected by our team. If we wouldn't stay there ourselves, it doesn't make the collection.

Personalised Service

A dedicated concierge plans every detail of your stay, from airport transfers to private chefs and yacht charters.

Local Expertise

Our regional teams live where they work. They know the hidden coves, the family-run trattorias and the right time to book Capri.

No Compromises

Transparent pricing, twenty-four hour support and a no-surprise guarantee. The villa you book is the villa you arrive at.
