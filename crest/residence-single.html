Private Amenities

Reserved for residents

A collection of private amenities accessible only to residents of One Crest Tower and their invited guests.

i.

Helipad Access

Rooftop helipad with on-call charter to JFK, the Hamptons, and weekend retreats. Two resident slots included.

ii.

Wine Vault

An in-residence 1,200-bottle climate-controlled cellar, plus a private bay in the building's grand cave.

iii.

Private Cinema

A 24-seat screening room with Dolby Atmos, first-run premieres, and a private chef's tasting menu.

iv.

Sky Pool & Spa

68th-floor infinity pool, hammam, salt cave, and treatment suites with cantilevered glass floor.

v.

Concierge

24-hour white-glove lifestyle service from the Aman group — anticipation as a standard, not a promise.

vi.

Private Garage

Four dedicated parking bays with private attendant, detailing service, and EV charging at every space.
