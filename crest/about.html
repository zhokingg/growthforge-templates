The Founder

An obsession with
the singular

Crest was founded in 2008 by Alexander Rothwell, a former private banker turned art collector, who believed the world's finest homes deserved the same discretion, scholarship, and care given to a great painting or a rare vintage.

Over sixteen years, he has personally represented more than 140 penthouse transactions across eight cities, counting sovereigns, founders, and fourth-generation families among his private clientele. Every residence in the Crest collection is inspected, photographed, and represented by him directly.

His philosophy is simple: take on only the properties worth obsessing over, and only the clients worth protecting. Everything else follows from that single act of restraint.

Our Philosophy

What we believe

Six principles that govern every residence we represent and every client we serve.

i.

Discretion above all

No listings, no advertising, no public disclosure. Every transaction is conducted under strict confidentiality, protecting both seller and buyer.

ii.

By invitation only

Our client list is closed. New clients are introduced solely through existing relationships, private banks, and family offices we have served for years.

iii.

Extraordinary, never typical

We represent fewer than twelve properties at a time. Each must occupy a singular position — an elevation, an aspect, a pedigree that cannot be replicated.

iv.

The view is everything

Elevation, orientation, and unobstructed sightlines are non-negotiable. If the sky is not the primary architecture, we will not list the residence.

v.

Service beyond hospitality

Our concierge is drawn from the ranks of the Ritz, Claridge's, and the Aman group. The standard is anticipation, not response.

vi.

A single client at a time

Each viewing is private. Each negotiation is personal. Mr Rothwell never represents two clients on the same residence — a rule without exception.
