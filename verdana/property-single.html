About This Unit

Unit 604 offers 750 square feet of thoughtfully designed living space on the 6th floor of Verdana. South-facing floor-to-ceiling windows flood the apartment with natural light throughout the day, while the open-concept layout creates a seamless flow between the living area, dining space, and kitchen.

The kitchen features quartz countertops, Bosch stainless steel appliances, and custom cabinetry with soft-close hardware. White oak hardwood floors run throughout the unit, complemented by a neutral palette that invites personalization.

The bedroom offers ample closet space with a custom organizer system, and the spa-inspired bathroom includes a walk-in rain shower, Grohe fixtures, and heated floors.
