Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Excellence

Founded in 1989, Estato began as a boutique agency in Manhattan with a singular vision: to elevate the real estate experience for the world's most discerning clients.

Today, our global network spans 40+ countries, with a dedicated team of 45 expert agents who collectively manage over $12 billion in property transactions. We combine deep market knowledge with white-glove service, ensuring every client receives the attention and expertise their investment deserves.
