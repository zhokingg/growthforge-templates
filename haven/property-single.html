One Manhattan Plaza

An 85,000 square-foot Class A tower on 42 Wall Street, One Manhattan Plaza was reimagined in 2019 with a triple-height marble lobby, destination-dispatch elevators, and a rooftop terrace overlooking the East River. Floor plates average 7,100 sqft with column-free eastern exposure, making it one of the most flexible layouts in the Financial District.

Amenities

Building features

Every floor at One Manhattan Plaza includes access to a shared stack of tenant amenities designed to make modern teams more productive.

Executive Lounge

A members-only lounge on floor 11 with barista-staffed coffee bar, quiet reading nook, and private phone booths for tenant executives and their guests.

Rooftop Terrace

A 4,800 square-foot landscaped rooftop on floor 12 with panoramic views of Lower Manhattan, open to all tenants with reservable event areas.

Conference Center

Four bookable conference rooms on the ground floor seating up to 40, with integrated AV, video conferencing, and on-site catering from Haven's partners.

Bike Storage

Secure indoor parking for 120 bicycles with repair station, adjacent to the tenant locker rooms and shower facilities on the lower level.

EV Charging

Sixteen Level 2 EV chargers in the underground garage, with dedicated tenant reservation windows throughout the business day.

On-Site Gym

A 6,000 sqft fitness center on floor 2 with cardio, free weights, and morning yoga classes — complimentary for all tenant employees.
