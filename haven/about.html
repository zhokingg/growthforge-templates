Our Story

From broker to data platform

Haven was founded in 2014 by a team of brokers who were tired of the opacity of commercial leasing. Sticker-shock pricing, hidden fees, and weeks of back-and-forth before a single tour — it felt broken. We decided to rebuild the process from the ground up.

Today Haven manages 2.4 million square feet across 240 properties, from Class A towers in Hudson Yards to boutique campuses in Austin. Every listing has transparent pricing, live availability, and a 360° virtual tour — so you know exactly what you're walking into before you book a viewing.

We still think of ourselves as brokers first. The data just helps us do the job better.

Our Approach

What sets us apart

Six principles that shape how we match companies to commercial space — from the first search to the day you get the keys.

01

Data-Driven Matching

Our matching engine scores every listing against your team size, budget, growth plan, and commute radius — so the shortlist you see is already the right one.

02

Transparent Pricing

Every property lists its true all-in monthly cost up front. No hidden service fees, no surprise CAM charges, no sticker shock when the lease draft arrives.

03

Virtual First

Walk through any space from anywhere with immersive 360° tours. Shortlist five buildings in an afternoon instead of five weeks of on-site visits.

04

Enterprise Network

We partner directly with the landlords behind every listing, giving our tenants access to off-market floors and early renewals you won't find elsewhere.

05

Dedicated Advisors

Every client is paired with a senior broker who stays with the account for the life of the lease — through expansions, relocations, and renewals.

06

Flexible Terms

From month-to-month flex desks to ten-year whole-floor leases, Haven's landlord network supports every team stage without punishing break clauses.
