heritage features

The details
that make a home

Every property in our care is distinguished by characterful features — from ancient oak beams to stone mullion windows, flagstone floors to walled kitchen gardens.

i.
Listed building status
Grade I, II* and II listed homes with full heritage documentation and advice on sympathetic restoration.
ii.
Original oak beams
Centuries-old timber frames, preserved and restored to their original character wherever possible.
iii.
Stone mullion windows
Traditional leaded glass in stone surrounds — period details that define English country homes.
iv.
Walled kitchen gardens
Productive kitchen gardens within original brick or stone walls, many with glasshouses and potting sheds.
