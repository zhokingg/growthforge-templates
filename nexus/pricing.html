FAQ

Pricing questions, answered

Everything you need to know about plans, billing, and what happens when you scale.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately and we'll prorate the difference.

What happens if I exceed my limit?

We never cut you off mid-request. Overages are billed at transparent metered rates, and you can set hard caps in your billing settings.

Do you offer a free trial?

Every paid plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. Cancel any time before the trial ends and you won't be charged.

How does annual billing work?

Pay yearly and save 20%. You'll be billed once at signup and once each renewal. You can switch back to monthly at any time.

Is there a startup discount?

Yes — we offer 50% off Pro for the first year for early-stage startups. Contact our sales team to apply for the program.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards, ACH, and wire transfers for Enterprise. Invoicing and PO-based billing available on annual contracts.
