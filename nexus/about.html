About Nexus

Building the future of AI infrastructure

We're a team of engineers, researchers, and designers on a mission to make AI development radically simpler. Nexus powers the next generation of intelligent products at thousands of companies worldwide.

Our mission

Empowering developers to ship AI faster

We started Nexus because we were tired of stitching together a dozen tools just to get a simple AI feature into production. Vector databases, model providers, observability stacks, evaluation pipelines — every team was rebuilding the same plumbing from scratch.

Today, Nexus is the unified platform that handles all of it. From rapid prototypes to billion-request workloads, we give developers the building blocks to focus on what matters: shipping great products their users love.
